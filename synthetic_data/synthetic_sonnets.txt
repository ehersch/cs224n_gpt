When shadows stretch and day begins to wane,

And memories of dreams take their toll.

Yet in this quiet contemplation deep,
A solace finds its footing, calm and sure,
As nature's gentle slumber starts to creep,
And promises of dawn we can endure.
So let us welcome twilight's soft embrace,
And find in fading light a tranquil space.
